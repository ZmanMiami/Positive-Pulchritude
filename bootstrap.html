Welcome to Positive Pulchritude exclusive (PPE)

PPE offers cool and exclusive perks that only a subscriber has access to. Exclusive perks span from secret merch drops to blog events where you have a chance to have your positive news featured on our site.

Insider exclusive

The insider exclusive perk gives a sneak peak of how we produce news, make articles and more! you'll even get to know what happens inside other countries that isn't on the everyday news.

Having the IEP you will get sent secret invites to meet and greets and journaling events.

Merch!

Now Positive Pulchritude already has amazing merch but having the merch perk you have access to custom gear, unorthodox merch that you don't see everyday.

We also do an inventory drop for merch that hasn't been sold so we don't waste clothing.

Community

We host art events, volunteer, and help the community. Our slogan: "Welcome to Positive Pulchritude, where we share uplifting news and stories to brighten your day!"
